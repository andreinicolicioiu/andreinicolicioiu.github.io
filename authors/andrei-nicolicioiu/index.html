Andrei Liviu Nicolicioiu

Machine Learning Researcher

Bitdefender, Romania

I am interested in making deep learning methods more robust out-of-distribution. I am a PhD student at Mila, previously having obtained a Bachelor’s and a Master’s degree from the University Politehnica of Bucharest. I worked at Bitdefender, Bucharest, Romania, and spend some time at Empirical Inference department of Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems, Tübingen, Germany.

I previously researched how to obtain models with good inductive biases for reasoning over entities and relations. My past work revolved around Graph Neural Networks and object-centric methods applied in the video domain. Recently, I have been interested in researching out-of-distribution robustness and making connections to disentanglement, object-centric methods, and causal representation learning.
