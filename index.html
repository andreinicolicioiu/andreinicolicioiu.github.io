Andrei Liviu Nicolicioiu

Machine Learning Researcher

Bitdefender, Romania

Biography

I am interested in deep learning methods with a focus on relational representations and graph neural networds. I studied at University Politehnica of Bucharest, where I obtained a Bachelor’s degree and a Master’s degree in Artificial Inteligence. I researched topics in multilabel classification, video captioning, occluded regions segmentation, few-shot learning.

Recently I have been researching graph neural networks and particularly how to design methods that take advantage of the relations in the video data and process them in a structured way and how to describe the long range connections of such methods.
