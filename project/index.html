Multi-label classification in video

Deep Occlusion Region Detection in Video

Developed a method for detecting occluded zones in a video using a fully convolutional model with three branches for multi-model input, with feature alignment by optical flow. The resulting segmentation maps are refined by a second, more localised convolutional network.
